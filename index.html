ABOUT

My name is Sang-Jin Lee and I am a 21 year old visual designer from Atlanta, GA currently pursing a BFA in Communication Design from the Sam Fox School of Design & Visual Arts. I love to know what ignites people's passions - what pushes people to and beyond their limits for the sake of their own sanity and work. I hope this site serves as a brief window into some of my own passions. Outside of design I love connecting with people; whether that be through conversation, musical performance, or design. In my free time I enjoy film, photography, dogs, and playing drums for my band. I grew up going to concerts - so music and performance are a huge part of my life.
